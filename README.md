# Black Holes Tracker

An Android Augmented Reality (AR) compass that allows you to point your phone at the sky and track the real-time location of known Black Holes across the galaxy and beyond!

## Features
- Real-time 3D AR rendering of black hole accretion disks.
- Tracks Sagittarius A*, Cygnus X-1, V616 Monocerotis, GRO J1655-40, M87*, and TON 618.
- "Show All" or "Show Selected" mode.
- Computes highly accurate J2000 celestial coordinates with Earth precession and atmospheric refraction corrections.
